Frequently Asked Questions

💳 Is this a real payment system?

No — this is a demo/educational payment system. No real money is charged. Use any test card number, UPI handle, or banking details.

🔄 Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your billing dashboard. Switching to Free is instant; paid upgrades activate immediately upon successful payment.

♻️ What about refunds?

In this demo system, you can cancel auto-renewal anytime. Your paid plan stays active until the billing period ends — then it gracefully downgrades to Free.

🌍 Why subscribe?

Premium plans unlock unlimited tracking, full Nova AI, advanced analytics, exports, and priority support — perfect for restaurants, hotels, or large families.
