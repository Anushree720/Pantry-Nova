How It Works

Three simple steps to a smarter, waste-free kitchen.

1. Track Items

Add your groceries with expiry dates, quantities and storage locations. Pantry Nova builds your smart inventory.

2. Get AI Alerts

Receive timely notifications about items nearing expiry. Nova AI suggests recipes to use them up creatively.

3. Save Food + Money

Reduce food waste, lower grocery bills, and shrink your carbon footprint — all while eating better.
